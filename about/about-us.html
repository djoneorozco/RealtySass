Our Proud History

Humble Beginnings...

Founded in Mexico in the 1970s by Isabel "Chabella" Rivera de Orozco. Orozco Realty began with a simple but powerful mission: to provide housing accommodations for individuals and families regardless of their financial circumstance. Driven by compassion and a deep sense of community, Chabella sought to ensure that everyone who needed had access to a place they can call home.
Over the decades, that founding philosophy has remained at the heart of OrozcoRealty. Today the company continues to uphold its commitment to housing equity while expanding its services and technology to meet the needs

Present & Future Vision

Legacy Build, Prestige bound.

Orozco continued the proud traditions set by Chabella over the decades. Constructing residences in South Texas and establishing business and services around Realty.
